About

Meet Catherine Powell

Catherine Powell founded CEP Equestrian Limited to bring together some of the world's most respected equestrian brands under one specialist sales agency. With years of experience in the equestrian industry and a lifelong passion for horses and riding, Catherine offers a deeply knowledgeable, personal approach to brand representation.

Working with retailers, equestrian centres, and event stockists across the UK, Catherine builds lasting partnerships between brands and buyers — combining genuine industry expertise with an enthusiasm for every product she represents.

From precision saddlery to performance apparel, safety equipment to technical accessories, CEP Equestrian is the trusted link between world-class manufacturers and the riders who depend on their products.
